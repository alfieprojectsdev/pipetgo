- Charge 5-15% of each quote value
- Example: ‚Ç±10,000 test ‚Üí ‚Ç±500-1,500 commission
- **Pros:** Aligned incentives, scalable revenue
- **Cons:** Need payment integration, escrow complexity

**Option B: Subscription Fees for Labs**
- Charge ‚Ç±5,000-15,000/month per lab
- Example: 50 labs √ó ‚Ç±10,000 = ‚Ç±500,000/month revenue
- **Pros:** Predictable recurring revenue

- Small subscription (‚Ç±3,000-5,000/month) + low commission (3-5%)
- Example: 50 labs √ó ‚Ç±5,000 = ‚Ç±250,000/month base + transaction fees
- **Pros:** Stable base + growth upside
- **Cons:** More complex pricing

### 2. How to Prevent Platform Leakage?

**Problem:** Clients might go direct to labs after first transaction (bypass your platform)

**Solutions:**

**Contractual (Cheap):**
- Non-circumvention clauses in Terms of Service
- **Cost:** ‚Ç±0 (just legal language)
- **Effectiveness:** 30-50% (depends on enforcement)

**Value-Add (Moderate):**
- Provide services labs can't replicate alone
- Examples: Multi-lab RFQ, escrow, analytics, reputation system
- **Cost:** ‚Ç±200,000-400,000 (Stage 3 features)
- **Effectiveness:** 70-80% (clients stay for convenience)

**Exclusive Contracts (Expensive):**
- Labs agree to only use PipetGo for certain service types
- Higher commission justified by exclusivity
- **Cost:** ‚Ç±0 (contractual)
- **Effectiveness:** 90%+ (but harder to recruit labs)

**My Recommendation:** Start with contractual + build value-add features in Stage 3

---

### 3. Pricing Transparency?

**Question:** Should clients see reference prices before requesting quotes?

**Option A: Full Transparency**
- Show reference prices for all services
- **Pros:** Faster decisions, attracts price-sensitive clients
- **Cons:** Labs might not like price visibility

**Option B: Quote-Only**
- Hide all prices, everything requires custom quote
- **Pros:** Protects lab pricing strategies
- **Cons:** Slower decision-making, higher friction

**Option C: Hybrid (Current System)**
- Labs choose: FIXED (show price) or QUOTE_REQUIRED (hide price)
- **Pros:** Flexibility, market will decide
- **Cons:** Inconsistent user experience

**System Currently Supports:** Option C (hybrid)
**Your Decision Needed:** Mandate transparency, stay hybrid, or force opacity?
